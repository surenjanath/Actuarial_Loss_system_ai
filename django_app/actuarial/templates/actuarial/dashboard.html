Model parameters

Weights should sum to 100% for a balanced interpretation; moving incurred weight automatically redistributes trend and reserve.

How the score is built

  • Loss ratio term — compares each year’s loss ratio to your target (capped) and scales by incurred weight.
  • Trend term — adds more pressure when the year-over-year direction is worsening vs improving.
  • Reserve term — inverse adequacy: thinner reserves push the score up, within a capped range.
